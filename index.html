Ragi Hospitality: Elevating Kerala's Hospitality Industry

Ragi Hospitality, a Cochin-based Hotel Marketing & Management Company, has partnered with prime properties like British County - Munnar, Crystal Cove - Thekkady, and North Seven Cochin. Executive Director Rethish Babu, a veteran with over 21 years of experience in Kerala's hospitality trade, founded Ragi Hospitality after serving as Corporate General Manager for Asset Summit Suites.

"Hotel marketing has seen a sea of change over the years, especially post-pandemic. Even though the markets have opened up in a big way, the guests are very demanding nowadays. With more and more options coming up, the competition has become fierce and competitive. Innovations and new concepts are the in thing that will keep us going," says Rethish Babu.

The vision of Ragi Hospitality is to have a property in all major tourist destinations across Kerala by the end of 2025 and to ensure repeat tourists from all over India into Kerala. To achieve this target, they will be making their presence felt at all trade shows across India.
