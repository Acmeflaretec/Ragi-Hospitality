Ragi Hospitality: Pioneering Excellence in Kerala's Hotel Industry

Ragi Hospitality, based in Cochin, is a premier Hotel Marketing & Management Company that has quickly made a mark in Kerala's hospitality sector. The company manages distinguished properties such as British County in Munnar, Crystal Cove in Thekkady, and North Seven in Cochin, ensuring top-notch services and exceptional guest experiences.

The Executive Director, Rethish Babu, brings over 21 years of extensive experience in Kerala's hospitality industry. Having previously served as the Corporate General Manager for Asset Summit Suites, Rethish Babu founded Ragi Hospitality to bring innovative and strategic solutions to hotel management and marketing. His vision and leadership are the driving forces behind the company's success.

"Hotel marketing has seen a sea of change over the years, especially post-pandemic. Even though the markets have opened up in a big way, guests are now more demanding than ever. With more options available, competition has become fierce and competitive. Innovations and new concepts are the key to staying ahead," says Rethish Babu.

Ragi Hospitality aims to have a presence in all major tourist destinations across Kerala by the end of 2025. The company is committed to attracting repeat tourists from all over India and making a significant impact at trade shows across the country. Their dedication to excellence and innovation positions them as a leader in Kerala's hospitality industry.
